About Us

Our Story

Systems are designed to protect and empower individuals, families, and businesses. However, many are often denied access to essential legal support due to high fees.

At Defaa, we believe it’s time to change this reality.

Every day, we dedicate our efforts and resources to simplify access to legal support so that everyone can focus on what truly matters—whether it’s caring for their family or building a successful business. Our mission is to make legal services accessible and affordable for all, offering our clients the security and peace of mind they deserve.

About Us

Defaa Law Firm is a specialized legal consultancy and advocacy firm established under the approved professional company system. It is licensed by the Ministry of Justice and registered with the Saudi Bar Association as a legal entity, headquartered in Riyadh.

The firm comprises a distinguished team of lawyers and consultants with extensive expertise across various legal fields, enabling us to deliver high-quality legal services with professionalism and excellence.

Defaa Law Firm combines local expertise with global standards to provide exceptional legal services tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.
